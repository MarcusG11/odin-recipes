Perfect Cast iron ribeye

Recipe for cooking a perfect, tender and flavorful ribeye

Ingredients

  • 1 inch thick ribeye
  • Sea salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • half stick of butter
  • 2 Tbsp of olive oil
  • Cast iron pan
  • Aromatics of preference (thyme, rosemary, garlic cloves, ect)

Instructions

  1. Rinse then pat dry your steak.
  2. Liberally season the steak on both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Add the olive oil to the pan bring to a medium-high heat.
  4. Add steak to the pan, flip after 4 minutes.
  5. Once a total cook time for 8 minutes has elapsed, add butter and aromatics to the pan.
  6. Baste (cover) the steak with the melted butter for one minute, flip the steak and repeat on the other side.
  7. Remove steak from pan, cover with aluminum foil and let rest for atleast 5 minutes.
